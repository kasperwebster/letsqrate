Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LetsQrate

How do customers access our digital menu?

Customers simply scan the QR code with their smartphone camera. No app download is required. The code takes them directly to your digital menu and other content.

How often can we update our menu?

You can update your menu as often as you like! Changes are reflected instantly. This makes it easy to update prices, add seasonal items, or remove sold-out dishes.

Do we need special hardware to use LetsQrate?

No special hardware is needed. We provide printable QR codes that you can place on tables, menus, or marketing materials. You'll just need a computer to access the dashboard and manage your content.

How does the loyalty program work?

Our loyalty program uses digital punch cards. Customers can earn points or stamps with each visit or purchase. You set the rewards and redemption rules through your dashboard.

Can we customize the look and feel of our digital menu?

Absolutely! You can customize colors, fonts, and layout to match your brand. You can also add your logo and restaurant photos to create a cohesive brand experience.

What kind of analytics do you provide?

Our analytics dashboard shows you how many people have viewed your menu, which items are most viewed, peak usage times, and more. Higher tier plans include more detailed analytics.
